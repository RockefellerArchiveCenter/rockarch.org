The Rockefeller Archive Center reading room will be closed until further notice beginning Monday, March 16, 2020. Researchers should feel free to contact Archive Center staff who will be working from home and who can be reached through the links on this site.

The Rockefeller Archive Center is a major repository and research center for the study of philanthropy and its impact throughout the world.

Remembering Tom Rosenbaum

Our cherished colleague, Tom Rosenbaum, passed away on September 16. Generations of researchers have benefitted from Tom’s thoughtful advice and wise guidance through our collections, and his devotion to the Archive Center was a gift to all of us. We treasured Tom’s broad knowledge, his intelligence, his active curiosity, and his sincere compassion. And we will miss him enormously.

Tom’s family is planning a memorial service at the Community Unitarian Universalist Congregation in White Plains, NY, at a time when such a gathering can be safely held. They have also asked that donations in Tom's name be made to the Unitarian Universalist Service Committee at: uusc.org.
